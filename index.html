Research

"My main goal is to understand human cancers from genomics and epigenetics data for developing effective targetted therapies."

My current focus is to understand tumors from evolutionary perspective and to develop algorithms for tumor evolution and progress.

Recent Research Highlights

  • Why do mutant allele frequencies in oncogenes peak around .40 and rapidly decrease? infers selection from mutation data and offers a mathematical model for natural selection. [PDF]
  • Applications: GPs for building design, Baysian optimization with UAVs, Automatic privacy permission for Smart-phones, Analysis of vote results, and Preference learning.

Current projects

  • Automating Data Science.
